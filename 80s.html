Windows 1.0
Early Beginnings

Created in 1984 for MS-DOS, Windows was the first and most primitive version of Windows; Being used as a Shell for MS-DOS and not having many native applications at the time, it did struggle to sell well and was often viewed to much of a power hog and more clunky then MS-DOS's Command Line. Coming bundled with some applications to demonstrate the usage of the mouse as well as the many graphical color capabilites that the OS could do.
