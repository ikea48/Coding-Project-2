Welcome!

Windows was created back in 1984 by a young Bill Gates and Paul Allen to compete in the newly created Personal Computer (or PC) operating system enviorment!
In this site we explore the history of the famous OS and how its gotten to today.

Windows (as most of us know) is a widely used OS in the consumer desktop market holding over 70% of desktop computer in 2023. Originally created as a shell for the at the time revolutionary MS-DOS which was a command-line based OS, it has since evolved into a large-scale Computer OS that works to run and displasy applications and provide ease of utility to the customer.

Windows has served as the primary platform for Home, Business, Software, and Hardware development platform since the mid-90's and has been keeping that title up until the most recent release of Windows 11. While competition has slowly been ramping up in the coming years, many other platforms will still have to wait to get to the level that Microsoft has achieved over the past 40 years.
